Meet Andrea

Experienced, encouraging, and grounded in real-life communication.

Andrea Scheppler is a pediatric speech-language pathologist who believes therapy should feel supportive, functional, and joyful. Her approach blends clinical expertise with warmth, helping families understand what’s happening and what to do next.

At Ophelia’s Speech Therapy, families can expect thoughtful assessment, individualized goals, and strategies that make sense for school, home, and everyday routines.

  • Child-centered therapy plans
  • Clear parent communication
  • Strength-based, practical recommendations
  • Playful sessions with measurable progress
